CaratCam™ Privacy Policy

Effective May 2025

CaratCam™ does not collect any personal user information. You are not required to create an account or share any personal data to use the app.

We do not store or transmit your photos, location, name, email, or contact details. All scan history is stored locally on your device unless manually backed up by you.

Scan limits (e.g. two free scans) are enforced using basic device-level identifiers stored locally. This data is never shared or sent to a remote server.

If you email us for support or request data deletion, we may view your email address and respond directly. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with anyone.

For any questions, contact us at: support@camlabs.ai

Data Deletion Request

CaratCam™ does not collect or store personal data by default. However, if you would like to request that any information related to your use of our software be removed, you may contact us directly.

To request deletion, please email us at: support@camlabs.ai

Include any relevant details (such as the email address you contacted us with) to help us process your request.

⏱️ Requests are typically processed within 30 days.

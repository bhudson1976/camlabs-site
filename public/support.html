📲 App Not Opening?

Make sure you've installed the latest version of the APK. Go to Downloads, tap the file, and allow "Install Unknown Apps" if prompted.

🔐 Purchase Not Unlocking Features?

Close and reopen the app. If that doesn't work, try uninstalling and reinstalling it. Still having trouble? Reach out below.

📸 Camera Access Issues?

Check your phone settings: go to Settings > Apps > CaratCam > Permissions, and make sure Camera access is turned on.
